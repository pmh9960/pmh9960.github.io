About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the Data and Visual Analytics Lab (DAVIAN) in KAIST AI, advised by Professor Jaegul Choo. My research interests include generative models, computer vision, and their applications. Recently, I have been focusing on applying generative models to robotics.
